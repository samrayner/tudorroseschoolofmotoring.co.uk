Welcome to the Tudor Rose School of Motoring, a professional driving school that has operated throughout Sutton Coldfield and the surrounding areas in the West Midlands since 1995.

I am Terry Jeffries, a Grade Four DSA approved driving instructor with an extremely high pupil pass rate. My aim is to teach you the skills necessary to maximise your chances of passing your test and to become a safe driver for the rest of your life.

I offer a full range of lessons that are all tailored to your individual needs. Feel free to contact me to discuss your requirements.

My Philosophy

It is true to say that some people will learn quicker than others. The Tudor Rose School of Motoring provides instruction to its pupils on a personal basis in order to reflect their own personal rate of learning.

It may well be that you have already achieved some of the skills required in order to pass your driving test. Should this be the case I can adapt your tuition in such a way as to focus more precisely on the areas requiring attention, thus saving both time and money.
